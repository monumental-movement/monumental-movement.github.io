description: "Explore how trip-hop — the atmospheric downtempo sound born in Bristol, England — evolved from underground experimentation into a globally influential musical language.
Emerging in the late 1980s and early 1990s from the city’s multicultural club and sound system culture, trip-hop fused hip-hop, dub, soul, jazz, and electronic textures into a uniquely introspective sonic form. This episode traces the sound’s origins, pioneers, stylistic hallmarks, and lasting influence on electronic and alternative music."
