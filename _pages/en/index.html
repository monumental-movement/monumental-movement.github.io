Monumental Movement

Monumental Movement is a cultural platform exploring music culture and the ideas and histories behind it. Alongside its role as an archive-driven media outlet featuring columns and podcasts, it also introduces curated used records and various media.

An independent archive exploring music culture, experimental sound, and underground movements.
