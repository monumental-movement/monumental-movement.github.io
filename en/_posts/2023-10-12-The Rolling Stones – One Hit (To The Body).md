"One Hit (To The Body)" is a single cut from the album "Dirty Work" released in 1986. Lyrics and music by Mick Jagger, Keith Richards, and Ron Wood. The guitar solo is played by former Led Zeppelin member Jimmy Page.

"One Hit (To The Body)" features an energetic guitar riff, Mick Jagger's powerful vocals, and a catchy melody, making it a classic for many rock music fans.

The A-side London Mix is ​​a remix by Steve Lillywhite, who was in charge of producing it.
