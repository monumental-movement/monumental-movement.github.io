Sami Koivikko is a techno producer based in Finland. Known as a standard bearer of minimal techno and click techno, he has released works on various labels such as the popular German label Shitkatapult, Spectral Sound, and Festplatten.

"Kut Pulatin Pt. 2" is a Techno/Minimal track released on the label Shitkatapult in 2002 and a piece that symbolizes the minimal techno movement of the 2000s.
