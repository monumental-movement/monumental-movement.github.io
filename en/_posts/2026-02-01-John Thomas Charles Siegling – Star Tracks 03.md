John Thomas is a French electronic music producer and co-founder of Ginga Music. He also worked as The Collective, a project with minimal electronic music producer/DJ Jean-Guillaume Cabanne and Jeff Lasson.

Charles Siegling, also known as Technasia, is a DJ/musician.

"Star Tracks 03" is a Techno/Tech House track released in 2001 on Star Tracks, a sub-label of the Hong Kong-based independent label technasia.
