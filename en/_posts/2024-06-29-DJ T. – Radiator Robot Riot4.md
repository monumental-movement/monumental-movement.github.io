Thomas Koch, aka DJ T., also known as Mathami, is a German DJ and producer. He has been active as a DJ since the 1980s, serving as a resident DJ at Frankfurt clubs such as Music Hall, Plastik, Dorian Gray, XS, and The Box.In 1989, he founded Groove magazine and ran it until 2004. Since 2002, he has been active as a member of Get Physical Music.

"Radiator / Robot Riot4" is an electro house/tech house track released in 2004 on the label 20:20 Vision. The B-side is a remix by Wesley Gill and Tom Taylor's UK house and electronic music duo The Electric Press.
