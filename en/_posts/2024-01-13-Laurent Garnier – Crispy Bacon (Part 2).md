Laurent Garnier, also known as Choice, is a French electronic music producer and DJ.

"Crispy Bacon (Part 2)" is a techno track released in 1997 by Laurent Garnier and Eric Morand's F Communications label.

The A-side is a remix by AUX88, an electronic band from Detroit consisting of TOMTOM (Tommy Hamilton) and POSATRONIX (William "BJ" Smith).

The B-side is a remix by Gilb'r Versatile, who runs the label Versatile Records.
