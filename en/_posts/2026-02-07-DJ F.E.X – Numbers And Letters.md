Farid Boudinar, also known as DJ F.E.X, is an Electro/House/Techno producer/DJ from Paris, France. He has released on many prestigious labels such as Systematic, Dessous, Pokerflat, and Safari numerique.

"Numbers And Letters" is a tech house track released in 2006 on Hypnotic Music, the TechHouse label sponsored by former Superfunk member Fafa Monteco.

B1 is a remix by German DJ/producer Martin Eyerer.
B2 is a remix by Spanish DJ/producer Loudeast (Ignacio Marco).
