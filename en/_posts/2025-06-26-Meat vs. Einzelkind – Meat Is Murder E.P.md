Carsten Schuchmann, also known as Meat, founded Freebase Recordshop in Frankfurt, Germany in the early 1990s, and has held Freebase-Tours and See Us There Festivals in Europe.As a producer, he is active on labels such as Get Physical, Klang, Kindisch, murmur, and La Peña.Since 2009, he has been working with Chris Wood as the DJ/producer duo Chris Wood & Meat.

Arno Völker, also known as Einzelkind, is a Frankfurt-based DJ and producer and owner of La Peña, Pressure Traxx (JAX), and Harlo.

This record is a Tech House track released by German electronic music label Get Physical in 2007.
