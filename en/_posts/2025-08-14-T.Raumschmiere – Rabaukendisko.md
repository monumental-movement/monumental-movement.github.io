T.Raumschmiere (Marco Haas) is a punk/techno DJ from Germany who is also known for his collaborations with Miss Kittin and Ellen Allien.

"Rabaukendisko" is a Breaks/Electro track released on the techno/electronica label NovaMute in 2003.

The B-side is a remix by Kevin Martings, aka The Bug, a veteran UK DJ/producer who has been active in the Dub/Jazzcore/Industrial Hip Hop/Dancehall/Dubstep genres for over 30 years.
