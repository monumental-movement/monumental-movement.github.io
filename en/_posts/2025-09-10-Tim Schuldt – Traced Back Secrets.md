Tim Schuldt is a Goa & psychedelic trance producer/guitarist/engineer from Germany who has been active since the mid-1990s. Originally active in rock and metal bands, their style has since evolved into goa trance and psychedelic trance to a heavier, industrial sound.

"Traced Back / Secrets" is an Industrial/Psy-Trance song released in 2002 on Tim Schuldt's own Goa/Psy-Trance label, Aurinko Records.
