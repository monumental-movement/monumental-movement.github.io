Digital Express is an Acid/Techno project by Jeroen Search, known as one of the most influential producers in Dutch techno, and Mischa van der Heiden (DJ Misjah), founder of Dutch acid techno label X-Trax.

"The Club (Remixes)" was released in 2005 on the Spanish label Blanco Y Negro.

A1 is a remix by Dutch techno DJ, Peter Horrevorts, aka Paco Lesotto.

A2hq, a remix by Michel De Hey, a Dutch DJ/producer who has been active since the early 1990s.
