Shinichiro Kono, also known as Ditch, is a Sendai-based Japanese producer who has been working as a Minimal House & Techno DJ since 2001. He has released on numerous labels including Minibar, Safari Electronique, Microcosm, Goosehound, Kalimari, and op.disc.

"Link Set EP" is a Techno/Minimal track released on the UK label Safari Electronique in 2006.
