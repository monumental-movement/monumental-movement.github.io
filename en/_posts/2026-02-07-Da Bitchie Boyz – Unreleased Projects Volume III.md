Da Bitchie Boyz is the duo of Fréderic Tharreau and Sami Zitouni Navarre. Fréderic Tharreau also performs as Beatbreaker, Bibi, One Slammin" Phrog, and Roger Murttock. Sami Zitouni Navarre is a French house/electro producer/DJ who also performs under the names Cuts"N'Drops, Norman Bridges, Sami Dee, and The Flamantic Orchestra.

"Unreleased Projects Volume III" is a House track released by the Da Bitchie Boyz label in 2000.
