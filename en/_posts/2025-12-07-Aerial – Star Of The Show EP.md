Aerial is a power pop band that has been active in the Scottish indie scene since the late 1990s. In 2000, he released his ""Signal E.P." on the London label Fantastic Plastic Records, and in 2001 he released his debut album ""Back Within Reach" on the Japanese label Syft Records.

"Star Of The Show EP" is included in the debut album "Back Within Reach", and the other three songs are not included.
