O.C.U. (Organized Crime Unit) is a hip-hop trio from Chicago consisting of DJ Tragic (DJ, producer), Homicidal (MC), and Sudden Deff (MC).

"I'm All That (You're My Everything)" is an R&B track released by the label Kapone Records & Filmworks in 1991.
