Eric Miller, also known as Thick Dick, is an American house DJ/producer who works under many names including E-Smoove. "Insatiable", released in 2002, is Thick Dick's masterpiece, reaching number one on the American dance charts.

A is a remix by Chill-Out/Balearic DJ and producer Chris Coco (Chris Mellor).

B is a remix by Swedish pianist and DJ Rasmus Faber.
