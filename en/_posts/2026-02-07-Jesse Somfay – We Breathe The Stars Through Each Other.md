Jesse Somfay is a producer from Canada who has been active with prestigious labels Archipel and Traum Schallplatten since the mid-2000s. His style is a fusion of minimal techno, ambient, and shoegaze. He is also well known for his activities under the name Borealis.

"We Breathe The Stars Through Each Other" is Jesse Somfay's signature Techno/Minimal/Acid track released on the label Traum Schallplatten in 2005. It is included in the album "Between The Dark And The Light".
