DJ Rok is a techno DJ from Germany. In the 90s, DJ Rok was the resident DJ at Berlin's first acid house club, Ufo, and the first resident DJ at Tresor.

"Silky" is a sample from Fern Kinney's "Groove Me" released in 1979.
