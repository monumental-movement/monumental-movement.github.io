Alter Ego is a German electronic music duo formed in 1992 by Roman Flügel and Jörn Elling Wuttke.

Released in 2004, "Rocker" became a worldwide hit, being played by DJs such as Felix Da Housecat, 2 Many DJs, and The Chemical Brothers, and was an Electro House track that became one of the dance anthems of 2004 in Europe.
