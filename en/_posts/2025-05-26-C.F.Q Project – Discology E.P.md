C.F.Q Project is the alias of British keyboard player, percussionist and producer Dave Rawlings.

Dave Rawlings was also part of the UK dance production duo Quartz with Ronnie Herel.

This record is a house/deep house/progressive house track released by the UK label Burning Records in 1992. The producer is Clubland.
