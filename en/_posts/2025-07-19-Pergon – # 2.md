Jürgen Driessen, also known as Pergon, is a German house producer. He has performed under many names including Bruce Wayne, Exit EEE, and Geoffrey Adams.

This work is the second in a trilogy released by Jürgen Driessen under the name Pergon. A techno/acid track released in 1994 by the German label Important Extracts.
