Steve Angello is a Greek-Swedish DJ, record producer, and remixer.

Steve Angello is a member of the Swedish House Mafia, along with Axwell and Sebastian Ingrosso.

The first track on side A is a remix by Swedish DJ and producer Eric Prydz, who also works as Cirez D and Pryda.

The second song on side B is a remix by Laidback Luke, a Dutch techno house DJ who has remixed various artists such as Robin S's ``Show Me Love'', Denki Groove, and Jark Prongo.
