Amel Larrieux is an American singer who was a founding member of Groove Theory along with Bryce Wilson in the mid-1990s. "Get Up" is a single cut from his solo debut album Infinite Possibilities released in 1999.

This record is an unofficial release of a House/Deep House mix by UK producer Guy Robin and DJ Leo in 2009.
