British-born Dave Ellesmere is a rock musician who played drums in British punk bands such as The Insane, Discharge, Flux of Pink Indians, Doctor and the Crippens, and guitar in Disgust in the 1980s, and currently works as an electronic DJ. He has also released under the names Voco Derman, Solvent Electron Industries, and Ronin.

"Four Symbols" is a Techno/Tech House track released in 2005 on the Dutch house label 100% Pure.
