DJ Fopp (Fortunato Gallucci) is a producer/DJ from Italy. His remixes include Delegation, Inaya Day, Robin S, Alfred Azzetto, Jamie Lewis, and Michael Watford.

"What You're Gonna Do" is an Italo House track released in 1990 on Inside, a sublabel of Italian label Media Records.
