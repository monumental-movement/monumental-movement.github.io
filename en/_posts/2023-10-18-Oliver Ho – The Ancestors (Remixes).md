Oliver Ho is a London-based DJ/producer. "The Ancestors (Remixes)" was released in 2002 by Fine Audio Recordings, a German techno label specializing in hard minimal techno.

The B-side is a remix by Ben Sims, a DJ/producer from London, UK.
