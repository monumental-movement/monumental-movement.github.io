Pink was a Japanese rock band that was active from 1983 to 1989. Pink's musical style has been described as a "stateless sound." The members are Yutaka Fukuoka, Atsunobu Yabe, who was in Haruo Chikada & the Vibratones, Hoppy Kamiyama, Hajime Okano from Tokyo Bravo, Steve Eto from Bakufujuu, and Hidehiro Shibuya from Chocolata.

"Soul Flight" is a Synth-pop track released by the UK label Rime Records in 1986. Pink's only overseas label release EP.
