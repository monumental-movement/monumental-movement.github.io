SUN Project is a Psy-Trance/Goa Trance project formed in Germany in 1996. The members at the time were Maik Hinkelmann, Marco Menichelli, and Matthias Rumoeller.

Psy-Trance/Ambient album (3-LP set) released in 1999 by Velvet Inc., the psy-trance division of Nova Tekk GmbH.
