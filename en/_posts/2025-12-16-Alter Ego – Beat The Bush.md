Alter Ego is a German electronic music duo formed in 1992 by Roman Flügel and Jörn Elling Wuttke. He is best known for his 2004 masterpiece "Rocker".

"Beat The Bush" is a Techno/Minimal/Acid remix released by German label Klang Elektronik in 2005.

A is a remix by German house duo Tiefschwarz.

B1 is a remix by Ewan Pearson, a remixer/producer from the UK who has remixed artists such as Cortney Tidwell, Seelenluft, Ladytron, Depeche Mode, The Rapture, Goldfrapp, and The Chemical Brothers.

B2 is a remix by UK electronic music DJ/producer Riton.
