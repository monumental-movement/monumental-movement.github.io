"Fly To Colors" is a song by German trance DJ and producer Markus Schulz. This song is included in his album "Progression" (2007).

"Fly To Colors" is known as a melodic and emotional trance track and has become one of Marcus Schulz's signature sounds. The songs are characterized by beautiful piano melodies, magnificent synthesizer sounds, and delicate build-ups and drops, giving the listeners emotional excitement.

“Fly To Colors” embodies Marcus Schulz’s classic trance style, with its energetic beat and epic soundscape. It was also played by trance DJs such as Armin van Buuren, and became popular on dance floors at clubs and festivals.
