Ursula Rucker is a spoken word artist from Philadelphia. She has collaborated with various artists such as Wax Tailor, King Britt, 4hero, Incognito, Jamaaladeen Tacuma, Josh Wink, and Alix Perez, and her collaboration with The Roots in particular made Ursula's name widely known.

"Circe", released in 1999, is a Deep House/Downtempo track produced by King Britt. A1 is a remix by Jazzanova. A2, B1 is a remix by Rob Yancey.
