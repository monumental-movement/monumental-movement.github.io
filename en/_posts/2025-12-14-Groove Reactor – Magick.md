Groove Reactor is a Belgian project by Maurice Engelen, Nikkie Van Lierop, and Oliver Adams. Oliver Adams is known as one of the most successful producers of the late new beat, hardbeat, skizzo and Belgian techno eras.

"Magick" is a Breakbeat/Trance/Techno track released in 1992 by the Belgian dance label Dance Opera, which was active from 1990 to 2003.
