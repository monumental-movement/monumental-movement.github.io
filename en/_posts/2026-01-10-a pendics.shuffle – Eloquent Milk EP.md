[a]pendics.shuffle is the alias used by Kenneth James Gibson, known as Dubloner, Bal Cath, and as a member of Bell Gardens, Furry Things, and Bell Gardens, to produce Minimal House, Techno, and Micro House electronic music.

"Eloquent Milk EP" is a Tech House/Minimal track released in 2006 on UK DJ/producer Matt Tolfrey's label Leftroom.

B2 is a remix by Ed Davenport, head of House & Techno label Counterchange Recordings.
