Anthony Wilson, also known as Ant and Chicago Loop, is a techno DJ and producer based in London, England. He has released on numerous labels such as Phobiq, Gate null recordings, and Renesanz.

"K.O. Computer" is a Techno track released in 2002 on Ant's own UK techno and acid techno label Power Tools, a sub-label of the label Stay Up Forever Label Collective.
