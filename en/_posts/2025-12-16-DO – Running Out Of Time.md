DO (Digital Orgasm) is a Belgian techno act formed by the M.N.O. production trio Maurice Engelen (Somora/Praga Khan), Nikkie van Lierop (Jade 4 U, Miss Lie), and Oliver Adams.

Digital Orgasm was one of their longest running projects, along with Lords Of Acid and Channel X.

A is a remix by Todd Terry. B4 is a remix by Josh Wink.
