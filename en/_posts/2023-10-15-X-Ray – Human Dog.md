X-Ray was a Japanese heavy metal band that was active from 1981 to 1986. Human Dog, released in December 1985, was X-Ray's last EP.

Members:
Akira Fujimoto (vocals)
Susumu Yuasa (guitar)
Takafumi Usui (bass)
Kazuhisa Takahashi (drums)
Takahiro Fujiyama (keyboard)
