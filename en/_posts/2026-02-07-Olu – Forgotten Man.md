Olu Rowe, also known as Olu, is a singer based in the United Kingdom. In the early 1990s, she debuted on Virgin's affiliated label 10 Records, which was popular at the time, and gained attention for her soulful vocals over house and R&B beats.

"Forgotten Man" is a House/Downtempo/Soul track released on the label 10 Records in 1992.

A2 and B1 are remixes by house music originator Todd Terry.
