Kenneth Lundgaard Bager, also known as Dr. Baker, is a producer and DJ from Denmark.He is regarded as one of the pioneers of Danish techno and house music, hosting the legendary "Coma" parties in the late 1980s.

This record is a techno/Euro house track released in 1990 by the UK dance label Desire Records.
