Jori Hulkkonen is a Finnish DJ/house producer who has been active since the early 1990s. He has collaborated with versatile artists such as John Foxx, Jose Gonzales, Jesper Dahlbäck (as Kebacid), and Phonogenic (as Discemi), as well as remixed artists such as Chromeo, Kid Cudi, Robyn, and Lydia Lunch.

"The Spirits" is a House/Tech House/Deep House track released in 1998 by the prestigious French electronic dance music label F Communications.
