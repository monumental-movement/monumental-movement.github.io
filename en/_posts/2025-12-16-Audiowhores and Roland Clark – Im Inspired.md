Audiowhores is the unit of UK producers Adam Unsworth and Graham Lord.

Roland Clark is an American house DJ and producer. He also performed under the names Jesus Jackson, Digital Pimps, Dark Clark, and Urban Soul.

"I'm Inspired" is a House track released in 2007 by British dance music label Defected.
