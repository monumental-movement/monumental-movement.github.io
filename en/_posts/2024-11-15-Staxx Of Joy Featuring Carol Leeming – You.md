Staxx Of Joy is a unit formed by house/garage producers Simon Thorne and Tom Jones.

Other names include staxx, Katch, So Damn Tuff, Tuff Boys, and Tuff Productions.

"You" is a house track featuring UK singer Carol Leeming. Released by Columbia in 1995.

The first song on side B is a remix by house DJ and producer Junior Vasquez.
