Go West is a British pop duo formed in 1982 by Peter Cox and Richard Drummie. Known for hit songs such as "We Close Our Eyes," "Call Me," "Faithful," and "King of Wishful Thinking," he was active from the mid-1980s to the early 1990s.

"Don't Look Down (The Sequel)" is a Pop Rock/Synth-pop track released in 1987, remixed by Bruce Forest from the 1985 original.
