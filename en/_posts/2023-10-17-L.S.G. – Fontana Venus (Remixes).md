Oliver Lieb, also known as LSG, was a central figure in the German trance techno scene in the early 1990s. Other names include Spicelab, LSG, and Ambush.

The A-side is a remix by Der Process, a unit consisting of German techno DJ/producer Good Groove aka Klaus Löschner and Markus Löhr, known as Hubert Kah's guitarist.
