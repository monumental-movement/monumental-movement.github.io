This is a single from "King Kobra", a project by his real name David Brian Srb.

David Brian Srb was born in Omaha, Nebraska, and is a multi-instrumentalist, singer-songwriter, recording engineer, and music producer.

Based in Italy from the 1980s to 1998, he sang many Eurobeat songs under various aliases.
He currently works as the Technical Director and Auditorium Manager at Christian Heritage Academy in Northfield, Illinois.
