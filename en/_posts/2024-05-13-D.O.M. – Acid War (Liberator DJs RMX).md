D.O.M. is a duo consisting of Jon Nuccle, one of the pioneers of UK techno music, and Mike Humphries, a techno producer who has been active since the early 90's. The two also work under the names Area 51, Cold Dust, and Drone.

This record is a remix of D.O.M.'s 1995 release "Acid War (Remixes)" by Liberator Techno DJs, consisting of UK techno/acid techno DJs and producers Aaron Northmore, Chris Knowles, and Julian Sandell.

Released on Stay Up Forever, a major UK acid techno label based in London, founded by Liberator Techno DJs himself in 2006.
