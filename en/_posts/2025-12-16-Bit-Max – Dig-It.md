Maurizio Pavesi, also known as Bit-Max, is an Italian producer/DJ. He has worked under various names including Italo Dance Project Alphabet, DJ Zanza, and Pavesi Sound.

"Dig-It" is a Techno track released in 1999 on the House label Beat Club Records in Milan, Italy.
