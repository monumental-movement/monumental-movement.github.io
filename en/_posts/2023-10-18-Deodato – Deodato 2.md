Eumir Deodato, also known as Deodato, is a Brazilian musician, composer, arranger, and producer.

Deodato's second album, ``Rhapsody in Blue'', released in 1973, included songs such as George Gershwin's ``Rhapsody In Blue'', Maurice Ravel's ``Pavane for a Dead Princess'', and The Moody Blues' ``Satin Night'' with Deodato's style arrangements.
