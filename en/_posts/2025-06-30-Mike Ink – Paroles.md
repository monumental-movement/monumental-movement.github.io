Wolfgang Voigt, also known as Mike Ink, is an electronic music DJ and producer from Cologne, Germany. In addition to Mike Ink, he is also known by various other names, including Gas, M:I:5:, and Wassermann. Since the 1990s, he has been signed to many famous record labels, including Warp, Harvest, Raster-Noton, Force Inc, and Astral Industries, and is considered one of the most influential minimal techno artists. In 1998, he co-founded the label Kompakt with Michael Mayer & Jürgen Paape.

"Paroles" is a two-disc set released by Warp Records in 1996. C is a remix by Auteka.
