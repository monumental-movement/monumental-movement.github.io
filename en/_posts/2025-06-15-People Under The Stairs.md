Re-edited by NY-based DJ bossy boots (Elinah). Unofficial release promo version.

Side A is a re-edit of "Tuxedo Rap" by People Under The Stairs, a hip-hop duo from Los Angeles that was active from 1997 to 2019, consisting of Christopher Portugal (Thes One) and Michael Turner (Double K).

The B-side is a re-edit of the masterpiece ""Garden Of Delights'' released in 1977 by The Last Poets, a group of poets and musicians born out of the African-American civil rights movement of the late 1960s.
