A project by Daz Saund, who was a resident at clubs Garage and Trade, and producer Ben Tisdall (Rizzo). The two also work under the name Housewerk, Sound Associates.

"The Chase EP" is a Techno track released in 2000 on Reel Musiq, a sublabel of Japanese label Sublime Records.
