Divine Inspiratio is a trance project by David Lewin, Lee Robinson, Paul Crawley, and Sarah-Jane Scott based in Fochabers, Scotland.

This record is a Trance remix of "The Way" by Belgian trance producers Svenson and Johan Gielen, released in 2002 on the UK label Heat Recordings. Promo board.
