Mr. Gelbeck, who unofficially releases high-quality house mixes, is rumored to be the pseudonym of a famous French artist.

Released in 2008, "Deuxieme" is the second in Mr. Gelbeck's unofficial house mix series.
The A-side samples Daft Punk's "Digital Love," and the B-side samples Sylvie Vartan's hit "Disco Queen."
