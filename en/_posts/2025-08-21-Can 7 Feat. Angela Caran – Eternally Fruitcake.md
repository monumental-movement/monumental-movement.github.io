Levent Canseven (Levent Can7), also known as Can 7, is a German producer/remixer/DJ who released many works from the late 90"s to the 2000"s mainly on Mousse T.'s label Peppermint Jam Records. Characterized by a sophisticated house sound that incorporates jazz and soul, he is one of the representatives of the European "lounge boom" of the early 2000s.

Peppermint Jam Records, released in 2002, is a DeepHouse track featuring house singer Angela Caran.
