The Siamese Twins are a duo consisting of twins Arndt Pecher and Markus Pecher. They were also the founders of the former Adam & Eve Records label, known for its jackets that imitated Tadao Yokoo's HAIZUKA PRINTING.

"Saladeater" is a masterpiece by The Siamese Twins released in 1993. Side A is produced by Arndt Pecher and Side B by Markus Pecher.
