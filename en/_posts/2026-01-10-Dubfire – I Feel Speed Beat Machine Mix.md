Ali Shirazinia, also known as Dubfire, is an Iranian-born House & Techno DJ/producer based in the US. He runs Electro/Techno"s digital label SCI + TEC Digital Audio. He is also active as the duo Deep Dish with Sharam Tayebi, and is well known for his remixes of De"Lacy"s ""Hideaway" and Dido"s ""Thank You." In 2006, he was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Dance/Electronic Recording for Deep Dis's ""Say Hello.''

This record, released in 2008, is an unofficial release of Dubfire's ""I Feel Speed'' mixed with Beat Machine Techno/Electro. (Single with A side only)
