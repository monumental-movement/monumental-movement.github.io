Ultravox is a UK post-punk rock band. The group's founder was John Foxx, who later left the group. Midge Ure joined in 1979.

Previous producers include Brian Eno, Steve Lillywhite, and Connie Plank.

"Vienna", released in 1980, is their fourth studio album, incorporating elements of synth pop and new wave. This album became a worldwide hit and featured Ultravox's signature songs and the band's masterpiece.
