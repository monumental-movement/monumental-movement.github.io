Baambata Flash System is an Italian artist also known as Sergio Flash and Steiv.

"Love Attack" is a Eurobeat/Hi NRG track released in 1989 by the Italian Italo Disco/Eurobeat label Asia Records.

The producers are Aston, Martin & Fadinger (F.C.F.), a team of famous Italo dance/Italo disco producers Mauro Farina, Florian Fadinger, and Giuliano Crivellente.
