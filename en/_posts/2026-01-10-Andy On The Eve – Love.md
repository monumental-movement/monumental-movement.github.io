Andy On The Eve is a project by famous Belgian producer Jean-Yves Vanloo, French DJ/producer Philippe Van Mullem, known as the producer of Quadran, and Thierry Traisnel. There were only two releases from this project: ""Melodies Of Passion" (1991) and ""Love" (1992).

"Love" is a Trance/Techno track released on the Beat De Ville label in 1992.
