Mr Hermano is a unit centered around Ben Mitchell and Chris Fitzgerald. Their style is a fusion of London dance music and Latin.

"Vasco Da Gama" and "Caxixi (Ka-SHE-she)" are Latin/Deep House tracks from the album "O Globo" released on the UK house label Disorient in 1999.
