Jody Watley is an American singer from Chicago. From 1977 to 1984, he was a member of the soul/disco group Shalamar. His solo hits include ""Looking for a New Love," ""Don't You Want Me," and ""Some Kind of Lover.''

"Whenever" is a House/Deep House track released in 2003 by Bill Coleman (aka Peace Bisquit) and Angelo Skordos' label Peace Bisquit.
