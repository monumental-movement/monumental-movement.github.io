4 Navigators is an Italian Techno/Hardstyle project. The members are DJ Vortex (Marco Paolo Pierucci), Marco Chiodi Corridi, Giuseppe De Iesu, and DJ Carlos, who are known as Italian hardstyle pioneers.

"Reiki" was released in 2003 on the Italian Techno/Hard Trance/Hardstyle label Stik Records.
