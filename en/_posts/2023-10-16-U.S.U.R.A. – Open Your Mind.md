"U.S.U.R.A." is an Italian techno music project by Claudio Varola, Michele Comis, and Elisa Spreafichi who were active in the 1990s.

Released in 1992, "Open Your Mind" is an early 90's techno anthem that was frequently played at clubs and festivals at the time, featuring a light beat that combines elements of techno, house, and dance, and catchy vocals.
