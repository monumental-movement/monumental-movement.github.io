PM Dawn is an American hip-hop and R&B group formed in New Jersey in 1988 by brothers Attrell Cordes and Jarrett Cordes. In the early 1990s, they achieved crossover success by fusing hip-hop, soul, and pop-oriented urban R&B.

Released in 1991 by Gee Street Records, this CD was P.M. Dawn's debut album that received rave reviews from music critics.
