"Sing It Back" is a song written by Irish-British electronic music duo Moloko (Róisín Murphy and Mark Brydon).

The song first appeared in its original version on their second album, ""I Am Not a Doctor'' (1998), and was released as a single on March 8, 1999, reaching number 45 on the UK Singles Chart. The song achieved chart success, reaching number 4 in the UK in September 1999 after being remixed by DJ Boris Dlugosch.
