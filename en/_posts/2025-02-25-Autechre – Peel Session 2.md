Peel Session 2 is Autechre's second John Peel session, broadcast on September 8, 1999, and a CD released on Warp Records in 2000.

When John Peel was first given this recording, it is said that he named the tracks himself, as none of the tracks except the fourth, ""19 Headaches'', were named.
