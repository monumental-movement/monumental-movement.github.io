Another name for Steve Miller, an electronic downtempo chillout producer from the UK.

He has also released numerous releases under the names Afterlife and Kid Stone, and has been featured on various labels and compilations, including the famous Cafe Del Mar.

"On The Ground" is a two-disc set released in 1994 with house, dub, future jazz, ambient, and hip house tastes.
