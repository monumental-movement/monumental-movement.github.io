Space DJz is a London-based duo consisting of British techno DJ/producers Jamie Bissmire and Ben Long. Ben Long runs the techno label Potential, and Jamie Bissmire runs the label 50 hz.

"AK-47." is a Techno track released on the label Potential in 1999. Although there is only one song on Side A, it actually contains eight short parts, each ending with a rock groove. The second song on side AA similarly ends with a rock groove.

#### Tracklist
```md
A. AK-47 (Lock & Load)

AA1. AK-47 (Wireless)
AA2. Shock Tactics (Initial Charge)
```
