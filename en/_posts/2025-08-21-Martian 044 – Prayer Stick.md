Martian 044 is the project name of Gerald Foster Mitchell, a stalwart of the Detroit techno and house scene. This name comes from Underground Resistance (UR)'s release number 044 = "Hardlife".

"Prayer Stick" is a Techno/Electro song released by Underground Resistance's sub-label Red Planet (distributed by Submerge) in 1998. Chuck Gibson's label Hitechfunk Productions is involved in the production.
