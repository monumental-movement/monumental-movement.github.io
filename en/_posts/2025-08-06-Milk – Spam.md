Kirk Robinson, also known as Milk Dee, is a New York rapper/producer who also works as the Hip Hop duo Audio Two with DJ Gizmo.

"Spam" is a Hip Hop track co-produced by Ad Rock of the Beastie Boys. Released in 1995 by American Recordings.

B1 and B2 are self-remixes by Milk Dee himself.
