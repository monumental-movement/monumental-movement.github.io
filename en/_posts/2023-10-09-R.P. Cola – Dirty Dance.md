He is a D.J., producer, former rapper, former dancer, and former choreographer from the North Side of Houston, Texas.

He has also appeared on many Screwtape soundtracks, including DJ Screw, Michael Watts, K-Sam, Lil' C, and the original underground film Hustle Town Mobbin'. In recent years, he has focused on DJing in local clubs, changing his name to "DJ R.P. Cola" or periodically "DJ Chill Neil."
