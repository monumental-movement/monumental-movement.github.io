Derb is a project by German DJ/producer Oliver Klitzing, who is active in the hard trance and techno music genres.

Released in 1997 on Belgian label Lightning Records BV's sublabel 4 DJ's Only, "Derb – Derb" is Oliver Klitzing's signature song and is known as a hard trance classic.

The first track on side A is a remix by German hard trance DJ/producer Frank Zenker, better known as DJ Scot Project, under the name Arome.

The second song on side B is a remix by DJ Merlyn.

#### Tracklist
```md
A1. Derb (Arome Mix)

A2. Derb (Dernimbus)

B1. Derb (Derbus)
````
