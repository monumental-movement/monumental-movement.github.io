NJOI is a British rave/techno band formed in 1989 by Nigel Champion and Mark Franklin.

The vocal front is Saffron. NJOI's songs had a huge influence on the UK rave scene at the time.

The second track on side A and side B are remixes by Josh Wink, also known as DJ WINK.

#### Tracklist
```md
A1. Bad Things (Original Mix)

A2. Bad Things (Vocal-ish Version)

B1. Bad Things (Wink's Stabbed Filled Acid Dub)
```
