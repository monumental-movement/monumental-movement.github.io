Six.By Seven is a British indie rock band formed in Nottingham in 1992. Original members are Chris Olley, Sam Hempton, Paul Douglas, Chris Davis, and James Flower.

Released in 1998, "The Things We Make" is Six by Seven's debut album. It includes the single "European Me," which NME described as "one of the best debut singles of all time."
