Collapsed Lung is a Britpop/rap-rock group formed in 1992. Best known for ""Eat My Goal.''

"Board Game" is a Dub/Acid Jazz/Drum n Bass song released by Deceptive, a label started by Steve Lamacq of BBC Radio 1 in 1996.

A1 is a remix by Ballistic Brothers, a project by Ashley Beedle, Darren House, Darren Rock, David Hill, and Uschi Classen.
