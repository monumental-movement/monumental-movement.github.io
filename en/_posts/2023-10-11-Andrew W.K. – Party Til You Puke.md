Andrew WK is an American rock singer, multi-instrumentalist, songwriter, and record producer who has been active since 1998. He is known for his work that combines rock, metal, and pop music with party-themed anthemic songs.

"Party Til You Puke" is Andrew WK's second EP released in 2000.

"Party til You Puke" was re-recorded and included on Andrew WK's first album, I Get Wet, released in 2001.

"Dance Party" was reworked into the song "Long Live the Party" for their second album, The Wolf, released in 2003.
