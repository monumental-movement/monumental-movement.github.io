The original "Time After Time" was released by Cindy Lauper in 1983 and became a worldwide hit. Dr. Money covered the song and re-performed it with his own style.

"Time After Time" by Dr. Money is characterized by an electronic sound that uses a lot of synthesizers and a unique vocal style. They reinterpret original songs and express them with new electronic arrangements.

This cover version preserves the beauty of the melody and lyrics of the original song, while allowing you to enjoy Dr. Money's unique sound and arrangement. Their covers have become an interesting piece for lovers of synthpop and electronic music.
