Jr. Walker And The All Stars was an American R&B/soul band that represented Motown in the 1960s. Junior Walker played as a saxophonist with the band Foreforier in the 1980s.

"I'll Go Where Your Music Takes Me" is a Funk/Soul track released by the 1980s-1990s soul and rap label Washington Hit Makers.
