The track was originally produced in 1994 and pressed on vinyl in 2015. "Josie's Jungle" is an alternate version of the track that originally appeared on Jet Star, with the Blackstreet sample replaced with Sugar Minott's "Herbman Hustling."

B-side is unreleased material. The number of sheets pressed is 100 sheets for the white label board and 9 sheets for the test press.

Producer [Uncredited] – Delroy Da'costa

100 copies pressed. 100 copies limited edition
