Peter Harris, also known as Blu Peter, is a British electronic dance music producer and DJ from South Wales.In the early 1990s, he held a residency at the famous London clubs Heaven and Turnmills, and in the late 1990s he was active in the nu-NRG/Hard NRG scene.

"The Pictures In Your Mind" is a Trance track released on the UK label React in 1996.It is one of the most representative songs of the UK trance scene in the mid-90s, and the B1 remix by Kadoc is still considered a classic.
