Timothy Blake is an Irish-born electro/house music producer/DJ based in Berlin, Germany. He has released works on independent European labels such as Kleine Reise Records, Dirt Crew, Fatty Fatty Phonographics, and Lectric Sands NY.

"Heat Crime" is Timothy Blake's masterpiece, released in 2013 on the German label Kleine Reise Records. (Limited press of 300 copies)
