Beat Reality is a Dutch Hard House project by Errol Lafleur, Mark Nieuwenhuijzen, and Rene van Oosterwijk (DJ Rene).

A is a remix by Dutch tech-trance DJ/producer E-Craig (Dimitrie Siliakus).

B is a remix by UK hard-house act Phlash!, consisting of Mick Shiner and Steve Hill.
