This album includes "Clash", which was also used in DJ Harvey's mix "Sarcastic Study Masters".

Equipment used: Emu Synthesizer, Moog IIIC, Prophet 5, ARP Odyssey, Oberheim 8 Voices, Yamaha SS-30, Yamaha CS-10, Polymoog, Yamaha CP-80, Hohner Clavinet D-6, EMS VCS-3, Pollard Syndrum, Roland Jupiter-4, Roland Vocoder VP-330, Roland MC-8, RolandTR-808, etc.

Original version from 1981 by Hideki Matsutake.
