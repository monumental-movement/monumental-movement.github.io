Eskimos & Egypt was a crossover band based in Manchester, England that was active from the late 1980s to the mid-1990s.

"Fall From Grace" is Eskimos & Egypt"s masterpiece, released in 1993 on the early 90"s UK house/techno label Deutsch Englische Freundschaft.

B is a remix by Beatmasters, a British producer trio that was active from the late 80"s to the early 90"s.
