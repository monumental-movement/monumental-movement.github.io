## Full story about UK techno innovators and their various projects

Text: mmrÔΩúTheme: Luke Slater = Examination of British techno pioneers and their music world

Luke Slater is a techno DJ/producer from Reading, Berkshire, England, who has continued to lead the global scene from the late 1980s to the present.
Utilizing numerous aliases such as "Planetary Assault Systems," "The 7th Plain," and "LSD," he has developed a wide range of activities from hard minimal to ambient, electro, and classical collaboration.

### Early background and musical formation

Luke Slater was introduced to music through the influence of his father, and became a drummer by the time he was 12 years old.In the late 1980s, he rose to prominence as a DJ in London's underground clubs.They made their debut with "Momentary Vision" in 1990 and have since become an indispensable part of the evolution of techno.

In the 90s, they released ambient to minimal works with Warp and NovaMute, and since the 2000s, they have been strongly connected to Berlin's prestigious club Berghain and the label Ostgut Ton, and have quickly become a representative of the European techno scene.

### Features of each project

---

#### Planetary Assault Systems (PAS)

This is one of Luke Slater's signature projects since the early 1990s.
Featuring a hard and minimalist techno sound, it is known as a symbol of "peak time techno" played in clubs around the world.

**Features**

- Hard kick and heavy baseline

- Cosmic and futuristic sound processing

- A long structure that leads to a trance state

**Relationship with label**

- 90's: Numerous releases from Tresor (Berlin's prestigious club/label)

- After 2000s: Moved base to Ostgut Ton (Berghain direct label) and continues to release works to this day.

PAS = Luke Slater's ‚Äúfront-line project for club hard techno.‚Äù

In contrast to the variety of solo acts and the ambient nature of 7th Plain, PAS can be described as a stoic techno expression that is purely meant to shake the floor.

#### The 7th Plain

The 7th Plain is an ambient/techno project started by Luke Slater in the mid-1990s.
In contrast to Planetary Assault Systems, which is characterized by a hard sound for the floor, 7th Plain pursues an introspective and floating ambient techno.

**Features**

- Dreamy and meditative soundscapes

- A fusion of Detroit techno lyricism and ambient

- Strong listening orientation, more like an ‚Äúimmersive experience‚Äù than a club

**Position**

A project that is Luke Slater's "other side" and symbolizes his experimental and poetic side in the techno scene.

#### LSD (Luke Slater + Steve Bicknell + Function)

LSD is a techno project formed in 2017 by Luke Slater, Steve Bicknell, and Function (Dave Sumner).
Each of them are veterans who have been active since the 90s, and through collaboration they pursue a hard and hypnotic sound.

**Features**

- Long and stoic minimal development

- Acidic textures and cosmic soundscapes

- Drive feeling that brings the floor into a trance state

**Position**

LSD is a "supergroup" in modern techno, a project that combines the experiences and styles of each member.
Live performances at Berghain and international festivals have received particularly high praise.

LSD = A ``culmination of hard minimalism'' brought together by three masters, incorporating not only techno but also electro, funk, and vocal songs, developing a wide range of expressions.

#### Solo name Luke Slater

Under his solo name, he is known for a wide range of works spanning techno, electro, and breakbeat.

**Features**

- Variety from hard and industrial techno to melodic and experimental pieces

- In addition to 4/4 beats, we also offer breakbeats and ambient music.

- Always balance club scene and artistry

**Position**

Slater is acclaimed as a flexible creator who crosses scenes, combining the hardness of techno with a pop sensibility.
It laid the foundation for later aliases such as Planetary Assault Systems and The 7th Plain.
