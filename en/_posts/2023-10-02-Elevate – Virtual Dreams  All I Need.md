Side A, “Virtual Dreams,” features a powerful 909 drum sound, followed by an impressive synth riff that leads into a trance-like, pad-filled breakdown.The synth riff then returns, leading into a powerful chorus.The second breakdown is even more detailed, with the return of the tasteful piano and lead synth with a more sophisticated melody.

Virtual Dreams is a powerful track and a must-listen.
