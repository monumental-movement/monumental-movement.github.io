Deepest Blue is Ministry Of Sound's electro/house project by Joel Edwards and Matt Schwartz.

"Give It Away" is Deepest Blue's signature song, released as the second single from their 2004 debut album "Late September."

B1 is a remix by Michael Woods, a producer/DJ/remixer of various EDM genres such as progressive house/electro house/trance.
