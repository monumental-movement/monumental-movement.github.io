KGB was a 1970s blues-rock group featuring American and British musicians Ray Kennedy, Rick Grech, Mike Bloomfield, Carmine Appice, and Barry Goldberg.

"KGB", released by MCA Records in 1976, is KGB's debut album. The producer is Jim Price, who has worked on records by George Harrison, the Rolling Stones, Eric Clapton, and others.
