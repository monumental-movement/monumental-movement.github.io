Axer is a DJ/producer duo consisting of Axel Hedfors (Axwell) and Eric Prydz (Pryda). Axel Hedfors
He is best known as a member of the Swedish House Mafia, and Eric Prydz was also a former member.

"123" is an Electro House/Tech House track released in 2006 on Pryda Friends, a sublabel of Eric Prydz's label Pryda Recordings.
