"Choice of a New Generation" is the second album by the punk rock band Fifteen.Released in 1992 by Lookout! Records.The album features long punk songs with themes of love and social commentary, and is similar to the band's debut album.

This album is also the only Fifteen album to feature Lucky Dog on bass and backing vocals.
