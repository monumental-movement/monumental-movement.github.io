Petter Nordkvist is a DJ and producer from Stockholm, Sweden. Their unique style is a mix of techno, electro, dub, ambient, acid, break, and deep house. He plays not only in Sweden, but also at Border Community events in London and throughout Europe.

"Untitled Acid EP" is an acid house/progressive house/tech house track released in 2005 on Deep Focus, a sublabel of Amsterdam label Deep Records.
