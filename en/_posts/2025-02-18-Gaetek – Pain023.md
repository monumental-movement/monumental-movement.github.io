Gaetek, also known as Gaetano Parisio, is a DJ and producer from Italy. Known as one of the founders of the Neapolitan techno scene, he has released on various famous labels such as Drumcode, Planet Rhythm Records, Primate Recordings, Zenit, Kombination Research and Mosaic.

"Pain023" is a techno/tech house track released in 2001 on Tortured Records, a UK label founded by Billy Nasty.
