O.L. is the alias of Oliver Lieb, who was a central figure in German trance techno in the early 1990s.Oliver Lieb works under a number of aliases, including Spicelab, LSG, and Ambush.

"O.L. – Feinmechanik / Knochenbrecher" was released in 2000 on Delirium Red, a sub-label of Delirium run by German techno DJ/producer Jörg Henze.
