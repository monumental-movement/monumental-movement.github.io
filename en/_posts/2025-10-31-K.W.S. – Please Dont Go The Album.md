K.W.S. is a British pop/rave band formed by producer Chris King, Winston "Winnie" Williams, and vocalist Delroy St. Joseph.

The album Please Don"t Go (The Album), released in 1992, is KWS"s only album and contains the KWS hit song "Please Don't Go" (a copy of a song by KC and the Sunshine Band that was covered by Italian Eurodance group Double You).
