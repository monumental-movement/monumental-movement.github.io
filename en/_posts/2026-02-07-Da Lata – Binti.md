Da Lata is a British group formed in 1994 by Chris Franck and Patrick Forge. Da Lata's music is characterized by an eclectic blend of Afro-Brazilian influences and the melting pot of his hometown of London.

"Binti" is a Future Jazz/Latin track released in 2000 by Palm Pictures, a label run by Chris Blackwell, the innovator who founded Island Records.
