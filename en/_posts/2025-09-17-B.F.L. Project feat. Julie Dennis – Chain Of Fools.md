A house mix of the famous song "Chain Of Fools" composed by Don Covay and released by Aretha Franklin in 1967, released by B.F.L. Project on the UK label Activ in 1998, featuring Julie Dennis.

A1 and B2 are remixes by Richard Dearlove, also known as Diddy.

#### Tracklist
```md
A. Chain Of Fools (Diddy's Tuff Vocal Mix)

B1. Chain Of Fools (B.F.L. Molten Mix)
```
