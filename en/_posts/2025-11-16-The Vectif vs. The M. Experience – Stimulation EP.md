The Montini Experience (The M. Experience) is a Belgian Techno/Hardtrance project by Bart Grinaert, Lajos Mészáros, and Marc Van Campenhout (Zzino). Bart Grinaert is best known as Housetrap, Sinesweeper, and as part of the duo Filterheadz with Maarten Wilmaers.

"Stimulation EP" is a Techno/Tribal track released by Belgian label Spectron Records in 1998.
