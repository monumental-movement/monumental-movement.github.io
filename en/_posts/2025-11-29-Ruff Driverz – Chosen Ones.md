Ruff Driverz is a UK DJ/remix/production duo consisting of Brad Carter and Chris Brown, active from 1998 to 2019. Known for hit songs such as "Dreaming" (1998) and "Waiting for the Sun" (1999), they also performed under the names 2000BC and Transit.

"Chosen Ones" is a Trance track released on the UK label Recognition in 2000.
