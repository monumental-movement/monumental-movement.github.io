    E-Rotic – “Willy Use A Billy… Boy”

    Sexy and catchy euro pop from Germany. As an interpretation of Hi-NRG from the 90’s onwards, this is a highly addictive track that will make you want to hum it.

    Tracklist

    A. Willy Use A Billy... Boy (Extended Version)

B. Willy Use A Billy... Boy (Safe The Sex Re-Max)

    Norma Sheffield – “Look Me In The Eyes”

    A piece that highlights the beautiful singing of the Italo/Hi-NRG project. Pay attention to the lyrical and sad chord progression.

    Tracklist

    A1. Look Me In The Eyes (Club Mix)
A2. Look Me In The Eyes (No Rhythm Mix)

B1. Look Me In The Eyes (FM Version)
B2. Look Me In The Eyes (Instrumental)

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Pleasure & Pain – Higher

    A project (1990-1992) by FRED VENTURA, a famous Italo producer who has been active since the 1980s.

    Tracklist

    A1. Higher (Club Mix)
A2. Higher (Bonus Beat)

B1. Higher (The Midnight Sun Edit)
B2. Higher (Instrumental)

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Max Coo & Steve Coo – “Boogie Dancer”

    Vocals by Ken Laszlo add spice. A good number that makes you feel Italo’s orthodox and groovy charm.

    Tracklist

    A1. Boogie Dancer (Woogie Version)
A2. Boogie Dancer (D.D.J. Version)

B1. Boogie Dancer (Rome Mix)

    Youtube


    Dave Hammond – “Africa”

    European-style Italo disco with a Eurobeat style. When you want to warm up the space with danceables.

    Tracklist

    A1. Africa (Extended Mix)
A2. Africa (Acappella)

B1. Africa (Radio Edit)
B2. Africa (Instrumental)
B3. Africa (Dub Mix)

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Pseudonym – “You Have The Right To Remain Silent”

    An important work involving Hi-NRG’s evangelist Ian Levine. A record that feels like a recording of the atmosphere of the club scene at the time.

    Tracklist
