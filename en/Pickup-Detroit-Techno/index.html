    Picking up works by Detroit Techno

    Pickup en 90s Detroit Techno
    31 Aug 2025

    “Detroit Techno works born from the gap between decadence and hope”

    Claude Young – The Dexit E.P.

    Features:: 1997, from the Belgian Elypsia label. This work combines respect for Detroit with my own sense of exploration.

    Recommended points: It has a unique twist on serious minimal development, and is a piece you can rely on when you want to maintain a tight flow in your set.

    Tracklist

    A1. Intro
A2. Dexit

B1. Prance
B2. Fade

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Model 500 – Deep Space

    Characteristics: Songs such as “Milky Way,” “Orbit,” and “The Flow” are characterized by floating synth work that feels like traveling through deep space, and spacey, hard rhythms.

    Recommended points: A masterpiece that condenses the future-oriented and cool sound of Detroit techno. Place it at the beginning of a DJ set to create a cosmic atmosphere on the floor.

    Tracklist

    1. Milky Way
2. Orbit
3. The Flow
4. Warning
5. Astralwerks
6. Starlight
7. Last Transport (To Alpha Centauri)
8. I Wanna Be There (Edit)
9. Lightspeed

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Model 500 – Flow

    Features: Minimal track structure with simple synth lines and rhythm. It is characterized by its spatial, floating synth work and cool, mechanical rhythm.

    Recommended point: A song that lets you experience the origins of Detroit techno. By adding it early in a DJ set, you can create a ““Detroit-like cosmic feeling’’ on the floor. Easy to listen to even for beginners, perfect for understanding the worldview of the Model 500.

    Tracklist

    S. The Flow M14 (G-Funk Mix)

T1. The Flow M26 (Underworld MK. 2 Mix)
T2. The Flow M38 (Alex Reece Mix)

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Carl Craig – Landcruising

    Features: Dramatic sound image that combines ambient, breakbeat, future jazz, etc. with a minimalist base.

    Recommended points: An album with depth and sophistication that takes the listener on an emotional journey. This is a work that both DJs and listeners will want to listen to carefully.

    Tracklist

    1. Mind Of A Machine
2. Science Fiction
3. A Wonderful Life
4. Technology
5. They Were
6. Landcruising
7. Einbahn
8. One Day Soon
9. Home Entertainment

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Ultradyne – Unknown Works

    Characteristics: Balancing danceable grooves and abstract experimentation. Track examples: “Tangible–Theory Proliferation (Ultradyne Remix)” and “Fantasy Era.”

    Recommended points: Perfect for listeners looking for an exploratory techno experience. Whether it”s on the floor or in headphones, you”ll be drawn to its experimental sound.

    Tracklist

    A1. Tangible–Theory Proliferation (Ultradyne Remix)
A2. Ultradyne–Fantasy Era

B1. Ultradyne–Society @ Work
B2. Ultradyne–War Drum - Live

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Drivetrain – Symphonium

    Features: Dark, melodious, energetic and danceable rhythm.

    Recommended points: Modern and highly unique sound. This is a song that will dramatically liven up the floor if you play it in a late night club or peak time set.

    Tracklist

    A1. Electronic Seduction
A2. Music

AA1. In The Rhythm
AA2. Soundtrack

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Jeff Mills – Very EP

    Features: A minimalist and tense piece that is perfect for club warm-ups.

    Recommended points: The simple yet gradually accumulating sound image tightens the floor, allowing you to create your own unique world view from the beginning of the set.

    Tracklist

    A1. Cobolt
A2. Untitled

B1. Cometh
B2. Untitled
B3. Untitled

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Jeff Mills – Lifelike E.P.

    Features: ““Condor To Mallorca’’ is iconic, with its calypso-like rhythm and keyboard resonance creating a unique floating feeling.

    Recommended points: With its trippy and spiritual atmosphere, this is a “magical piece” that instantly changes the atmosphere when inserted into a DJ set.

    Tracklist

    A1. Condor To Mallorca
A2. Time After Space

B1. Detached
B2. Black Avenger

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Jeff Mills – Skin Deep EP

    Features: The track “Call Of The Wild” quotes percussion from Michael Jackson’s “Off The Wall”.

    Recommended points: The hard feel of Detroit techno is combined with the groove of black music, giving the floor a unique driving force.

    Tracklist

    A1. Call Of The Wild
A2. Skin Deep

B1. L8
B2. Scout

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Jeff Mills – Circus

    Features: An EP that contains 2 tracks and creates a hard, industrial atmosphere.

    Recommended point: Its intense sound pressure has the power to explode the floor at once when used during peak times.

    Tracklist

    A. Circus (The Greatest Show On Earth Mix)

B. Circus (The Big Finale Mix)

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    “M” – More Drama (under Axis name)

    Features: Track released under the name “M” by Axis in 1992. A song that represents early Detroit minimal.

    Recommended points: A must-have disc for collectors who can experience the underground feel of the early 90’s, with a rough yet sturdy feel.

    Tracklist

    A1. Scenario
A2. Switchblade

B1. The Fuzz (Loophole Mix)
B2. Getaway

    Purchase on Mercari
