    “Beat that penetrates the night, the soul of Berlin, German Techno works”

    Tresor Compilation Vol. 7

    Attractive point: A compilation by the prestigious German label Tresor, with a luxurious selection of tracks from some of the biggest names in techno, including Sven Väth, Maurizio, Ben Sims, James Ruskin, and Pacou. This is a piece that allows you to fully experience the charm of the hard, minimal, and innovative techno that symbolizes Berlin-Germany.

    Tracklist

    1. Sven Väth –Dub Control Your Mind (Track &amp; Feel Edit)
2. Maurizio –M4
3. Infiniti –Walking On Water
4. Ben Sims –Theoretical
5. DJ Shufflemaster –Innervisions
6. James Ruskin –Indirect World
7. Karl O'Connor –Guiltless
8. Sterac –Rolling Thunder
9. Pacou –It's Jazzy
10. Rok –Dance With A Stranger
11. Daniel Bell –The Butterfly Effect
12. Savvas Ysatis –Alright (Surgeon's Keith 4 Nat Mix)

    Zombie Nation – Souls At Zero

    Attractive point: A masterpiece from Zombie Nation, a project by German DJ/producer Splank! (Florian Senfter). In particular, the A-side “Souls At Zero (Original Album Version)” is paired with a remix B-side by Sven Väth. The remix by Sven Väth has a powerful finish that combines the heaviness and drama of German techno.

    Tracklist

    A. Souls At Zero (Original Album Version)

B. Souls At Zero (Sven Väth Remix)

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Cygnus X – The Orange Theme (The Remixes)

    Attractive point: A remix of the energetic and uplifting trance techno masterpiece “The Orange Theme” by German unit Cygnus X. Contains a variety of remixes by Moonman, Bervoets & De Goeij, Mark Van Dale with Enrico, and more, allowing you to enjoy club-friendly arrangements that capture the essence of the original song.

    Tracklist

    A1. The Orange Theme (Moonman's Orange Juice Mix)
A2. The Orange Theme (Original Mix)6:29

B1. The Orange Theme (Bervoets &amp; De Goeij Remix)
B2. The Orange Theme (Mark Van Dale With Enrico Mix)

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Elektrochemie LK – Gold!

    Attractive point: A 2001 release by Thomas Schumacher’s solo project “Elektrochemie LK.” An attractive piece that combines a solid beat with a classic German techno aesthetic. The free-spirited and edgy track development shines.

    A1. Schall (Album Version)

    Elektrochemie LK – When I Rock

    Attractive point: “When I Rock” is one of Thomas Schumacher’s signature tracks. In addition to the original mix, it includes remixes by DJ Rush and Santos, making it a work where you can enjoy the clash of rhythms and the richness of different interpretations.

    Tracklist

    A1. When I Rock (Original Mix)
A2. When I Rock (DJ Rush's Rock Da Beat Remix) 

B1. When I Rock (Santos Remix)

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Bad Pimps – Give It

    Attractive point: A project by German Kanzleramt label founder Patrick Lindsey and Belgian techno artist Stanny Franssen (G-Force). Released in 2002 by Monika Kruse’s label Terminal M, this is an exciting crossover between Techno and Tech House.

    Tracklist

    A. Give.It.!

B1. Move It In A Dirty Way But Maybe That's To Long Monika?
B2. Get On Up!

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Bazz-Dee & Waldhaus Weichentechnikk – Brachial 200404

    Attractive point: Hard techno with a strong industrial flavor by Waldhaus and Bazz-Dee, including a track that boldly samples KISS’s “I Was Made For Loving You.” As the title suggests, the “brachial” (radical and crude) style is irresistible to fans.

    Tracklist

    A1. Bazz-Dee &amp; Waldhaus – I Was Made For Loving Your

A2. Bazz-Dee &amp; Weichentechnikk – Samurai Pizza Cats

    Purchase on Mercari

    Youtube


    Johannes Heil – Future Primitive

    Attractive point: A work by Johannes Heil, characterized by dark and dramatic direction. A piece that invites a futuristic techno experience with deep beats and a psychedelic approach. The story is impressive as it moves back and forth between silence and explosion.

    Tracklist

    A1  Future Primitive Intro
A2  Children Of The Night
