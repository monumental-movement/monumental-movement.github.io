    Binary Finary – 2000

    Vinyl en Hard Trance Trance
    14 Oct 2023

    Binary Finary is a UK trance music duo formed by Matt Rankin and Stuart Matheson. They were active in the 1990s and released many songs that are known as trance classics.

    Released in 2000, “2000” was frequently played in clubs and festivals and was a huge success in the trance scene at the time.

    The B-side is a remix by Ferry Corsten and DJ Tiësto’s Dutch trance project Gouryella.
